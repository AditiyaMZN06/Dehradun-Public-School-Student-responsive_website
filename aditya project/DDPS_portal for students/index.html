About Us

Established in 1983, Dehradun Public School — popularly known as DDPS — is a trusted name in education, with campuses in Ghaziabad and Muzaffarnagar, Uttar Pradesh.
As a co-educational, CBSE-affiliated institution, DDPS offers a nurturing and future-ready learning environment for students from Pre-School to Class XII. Over the decades,
we’ve grown to become one of the city’s largest and most respected schools, known for consistently blending academic excellence with vibrant co-curricular achievements.
At DDPS, we are committed to shaping confident, compassionate, and capable learners for tomorrow.
